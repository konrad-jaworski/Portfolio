## Projects

### Anthropomorphic Robotic Structure

Biologically inspired robotic structure designed to represent different human body configurations with modular scalability.

**Highlights:**
- Human-inspired kinematics translated into mechanical design  
- Modular joint architecture enabling rapid iteration and reconfiguration  
- Complex structure implemented in a compact and adaptable form  

---

### Cycloidal Gearbox

Compact, high-ratio gearbox designed for robustness and manufacturability.

**Highlights:**
- High torque transmission in a compact form factor  
- Modular configuration of gear ratios  
- Designed for 3D printing and rapid prototyping  
- Geometry optimized for robotic applications and cable management  

---

### 6-Axis Robotic Arm

Full 6-DOF robotic manipulator built as a test platform for robotics systems.

**Highlights:**
- Complete kinematic chain design  
- Structural optimization for stiffness and repeatability  
- Integration-ready architecture for actuators and sensors  
- Expandable toward control and perception systems  

---

### Thermography & Hyperspectral Imaging System

Custom-built experimental platform for thermal and spectral data acquisition.

**Highlights:**
- Active thermography with controlled excitation  
- Hyperspectral imaging using motorized filter wheels  
- Synchronization between PC, embedded systems, and sensors  
- Custom data acquisition and processing pipelines
